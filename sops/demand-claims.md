## Purpose

Provide a clear, standardized process for creating and submitting **Demand Claims** to secondary or subsequent payers when normal claim processing cannot be used.

## In Scope

- Creating Demand Claims after primary adjudication when all mandatory validations are met
- Documentation and audit trail requirements
- Handoff to secondary follow-up processes

## Out of Scope

- Routine secondary billing (use standard claim submission)
- Primary payer claim corrections
- Full denial management or appeals

## Roles

| Role | Responsibilities |
|------|------------------|
| Registration / Billing Specialist | Perform validations, prepare and submit Demand Claim, document rationale |
| Supervisor / COB Specialist | Escalate and approve complex or unclear cases |

## Prerequisites

**Mandatory Validations (All 3 must be true)**:
1. Primary payer has already paid or fully processed the claim (adjudication complete; payment posted or denial received and worked).
2. Demand Claim is required specifically to prevent duplicate claim submission to the primary payer.
3. Secondary (or next responsible party) billing is needed per the current Visit Filing Order.

## Step-by-Step Procedure

1. **Identify Demand Claim Trigger**  
   Receive alert from work queue, VFO update, or denial review.

2. **Perform Mandatory Validations**  
   Confirm all three prerequisites are met. If not, stop and refer to appropriate process (Registration or Visit Filing Order SOP).

3. **Verify Primary Adjudication**  
   Confirm payment or denial is fully posted in the system.

4. **Confirm Visit Filing Order**  
   Validate that the current VFO requires billing the next payer with remaining liability.

5. **Prepare Demand Claim**  
   Use system function to generate the Demand Claim for the secondary payer.

6. **Document Thoroughly**  
   Add detailed account note including:
   - All three mandatory validations
   - Specific rationale and sources
   - Date/time of action

7. **Submit Demand Claim**  
   Submit to the secondary payer.

8. **Flag for Follow-Up**  
   Route to secondary adjudication work queue or NRP process.

## Quality Checks

- All 3 mandatory validations documented
- Strong, traceable account note present
- No risk of duplicate primary billing
- Escalation performed if validations unclear

## Optimization Notes

- Treat as exception process only
- Focus on clear decision gates to reduce mental friction
- Strong documentation protects against audit risk

## Regulatory / Compliance Notes

- Demand Claims must comply with payer timely filing requirements and Coordination of Benefits rules.
- Maintain audit-ready documentation for all actions.
